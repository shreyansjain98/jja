About Us

J. Jain & Associates, Chartered Accountants is a reputed Chartered Accountancy firm with over 35 years experience in the field of Accounts, Audits, Direct and Indirect Taxation, Management Consultancies, Anti Dumping Duties, etc. The firm was started as a sole proprietorship firm by Mr. Jinesh Kumar Jain in a small office with limited client base, which was converted into a partnership concern with the admission of Mr. Rishabh Jain as a partner into the firm. We cater to family owned businesses, MNC’s, Listed companies, start-ups, venture capital funds to provide a holistic and a world class solutions to the acute problems/ difficulties faced by businesses in the ever evolving legal world. We believe in having a long lasting relation with our clients, who have always valued our holistic approach to the problems and complexities of various business issues, in effect helping them take informed business decisions to manage their financial affairs. A well organized, experienced and dynamic team, with specialized networking partners, with a low turn-around time help us offer the clients the best advise to their various complex business decisions.

Team

Jinesh Jain

CA, B.Com. (Hons)

Mr. Jinesh Jain is a fellow member of the Institute Of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI), and a qualified DISA and a qualified Insolvency Professional.

Mr. Jain is well known among the professional circles and is a tax committee member of various chamber of commerce in the city, such as Calcutta Chamber of Commerce and Mercantile Chamber of Commerce. Having over 3 decades of experience in the field of finance, audit, taxation and consultancy, he has advised clients on various legal and taxation matters. He has advised clients on various investment related matters and has a deep understanding of the assurance services

Rishabh Jain

CA, CFA, B.Com. (Hons)

Mr. Rishabh Jain is an Associate Member of the Institute Of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI), and a qualified Chartered Financial Analyst, (CFA, USA).

Rishabh Jain (Partner) He has worked as a consultant in the transaction tax department of EY, and was involved in the structuring and implementation of amalgamation/ merger of MNC’s as well as Indian companies.
